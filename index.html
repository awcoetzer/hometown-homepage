Top three activities to do in Plovdiv

Shopping

Visit some of the local shops while taking a walk though Europe's longest pedestrian zone located on the main street of Plovdiv.

Beautiful Parks

Visit the local park, where you can find groups of locals playing chess, roller blading or enjoying the various water fountains.

Amphitheater

Take a tour through the Roman theatre of Philippopolis, dated as far back as the 1st century AD. Located in the city center.

Lots Of Museums

Reginal Ethnographic Museum

Visit the Kuyumdzhioglu House, the museum's home, which was built in 1847 for the Plovdiv merchant, Argir Hristov Kuyumdzhioglu. Each room holds a different exhibit, showcasing the the prime example of Plovdiv's mid 19th century architecture.
